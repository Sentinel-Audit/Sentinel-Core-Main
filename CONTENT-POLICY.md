# What belongs in this public repo (and what does not)

## Intended contents

- Original or lightly edited **Markdown articles** (security concepts, development practices, tooling).
- **High-level, non-client-specific** summaries of finding themes (e.g. from public contests or anonymised patterns).
- **Curated link lists** and short notes pointing to open-source projects, documentation, and courses.
- Small **development** examples (e.g. cron, CI snippets) that contain no secrets.

## Do not publish here

- **Private or NDA client work**, identifiable production code, or deployment details from engagements.
- **Proprietary or copyrighted** third-party audit text pasted in full without permission.
- **Secrets**: API keys, tokens, `.env`, private keys, internal URLs, employee PII.
- **Sentinel-RWA** (or similar) **internal** specs, unreleased contract source, or deployment parameters intended to stay private.
- Large **vendored clones** of upstream courses or repos when a **link + short description** is enough (respect licenses and repo size).

When in doubt, publish a **summary + link** instead of copying whole trees.
